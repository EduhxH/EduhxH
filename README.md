## Eduardo Carvalho

18-year-old developer focused on **backend systems and AI pipelines**. I build REST APIs with proper auth, validation, and database layers — and I apply the same engineering mindset to AI: full RAG pipelines from document ingestion to streamed response, running entirely on local models.

My primary interest is understanding how systems work at each layer, not just making them run. I care about architectural decisions: why Zod at the boundary, why MD5 deduplication before re-indexing, why a relevance threshold before generation.

Currently expanding into **C++** and **Java** alongside ongoing AI/agent development.
